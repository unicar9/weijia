/About Me

I'm a newly graduated General Assembly student. Previously I worked as a graphic designer and had a general interest towards computer related stuff. I learned programming through some online courses before, but in order to enter this industry and make some contribution, it's more sensible to obtain some systematic training through formal institutions. After three months, I love coding and building websites even more as I developed more skills with it. I especially enjoyed the process of doing every individual or project project, because no matter how frustrated and anxious I felt along the way, there's always a huge sense of fulfilment at the end.

Now I'm looking for a full-time position to utilise what I learned and keep learning. My goal is make dynamic, user-friendly, functional yet visually pleasing websites.
